About

Hey, It's Jungsoo!

I have completed my Master’s Degree in Genomics Data Analysis from Korea University College of Medicine. I like challenges, love to learn new technologies, and constantly develop my skills.


Main Research Experiences

Cancer Genomics

I have analyzed uterine sarcoma samples using comprehensive NGS platforms including WES, RNA-Seq, and WGS. The various data analyses that I’ve conducted are as follows:

  • Investigation of somatic mutation landscape
  • Detection of cancer driver genes
  • Analysis of copy number variation
  • Inference of transcription factor activity
  • Detection of recurrent gene fusions and structural variations

Single-cell/nucleus and Spatial Transcriptomics

I had a chance to participate in analyzing the ESCC samples with three transcriptomic platforms. Some of the analyses that I’ve experienced are as follows:

  • Detection of doublets and multiplets
  • Correction of batch effects between scRNA-seq and snRNA-seq
  • Cell type annotation with DEGs and unsupervised clustering
  • Pseudotime trajectory analysis
  • Decomposing and integrating spatial transcriptomics using single-cell/nucleus data
